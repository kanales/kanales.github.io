Mysterious Maps

The head of the Geometry department is well known for her rather
cryptic invitations to events. This year, you got an invitation for a
riverside picnic, but unsurprisingly it does not tell you
where.

Instead, you received a letter with the following instructions:

Come have fun with us! We will be at the intersection of the river with the vertical line that **minimizes the purple areas**.

Attached to it, you found a plot:

<img src="/river.png" id="image" />

Fortunately, you were given the function of the river as f(x)=12πe−x2/2.

You also know that knowing the y-axis coordinate is enough
provided you know it exactly: you can
simply start walking until you intersect the river. Knowing
all this, what is the y coordinate of the picnic location?
